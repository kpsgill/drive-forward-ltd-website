Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Drive Forward, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information you provide to us when using our business services. By engaging with Drive Forward, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, physical address, and other contact details.
  • Business Information: Company name, business address, industry, and details related to the business services you require.
  • Financial Information: (If applicable for invoicing/payroll services) Bank account details, tax information, and payment history.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage data when you interact with our website or digital platforms.
  • Communication Data: Records of your correspondence with us, including emails and messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage the business services you request.
  • To communicate with you regarding your services, inquiries, and updates.
  • To process transactions and send invoices.
  • To improve our services and tailor them to your needs.
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.
  • For internal record keeping and business purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes using secure servers, encryption, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.
